Golden Ears Peak

Golden Ears is a summit in British Columbia that stands at a whooping 1716m above sea level.

Golden Ears and I have a special relationship because I can see it from my house in Coquitlam.

Here are some things I love about this peak:

  • It's a beautiful thing to watch the sun rise over
  • It's always snowy
  • You have an amazing view from the summit
  • It's an excellent workout to get to the top (takes 10-12 hours round trip to summit)
